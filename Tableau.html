TABLEAU - E-Commerce CUSTOMER ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

This is an introduction on my Tableau dashboard.
Please visit my Tableau Public page at here to look for the dashboard.

Note: Please note that this dashboard utilized the analyzed dataset from my SQL project. You may check out the content of my SQL project at here if you want to.

The data source is from a public dataset of sales orders made in a Brazilian e-commerce franchise named Olist Store from 2016 to 2018. The dataset is downloaded from Kaggle.

In this project, the key objective is to visualize the pattern and trends of customer behavior and explore the relationship between different dimensions using an interactive dashboard.

1. Distribution by state: Analyzing the distribution of customers across different states
2. Customer segmentation: Utilizing the RFM Model to categorize customers into distinct segments
3. Monthly orders trend: Analyzing the distribution of customer orders across different months
4. Distribution by payment value: Analyzing the distribution of customers based on payment value ranges
